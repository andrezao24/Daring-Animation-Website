Brand Guidelines

1.0 Brand Introduction

Daring Animation specializes in the creation of cinematic works, focusing on developing empathetic characters in fictional stories.

Brand Slogan

"Born to Create" conveys originality and artistic expression. Supported by: "Are you bold enough?"

Brand Vision

To create unique, empathetic characters within emotionally engaging stories.

Core Values

Audacity

Creative boldness and willingness to take risks.

Creativity

Exploring original ideas and innovative visual solutions.

Empathy

Creating emotionally engaging characters and stories.

2.0 Brand Logo & Concept

The brand's angular geometry and sharp edges project a deliberate boldness by negating rounded corners. Our skull mascot, Spike, signifies rebellion and fearlessness through an animalistic jaw inherited from the parent brand.
