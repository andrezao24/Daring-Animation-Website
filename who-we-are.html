DARING ANIMATION

"Born to Create"

Daring Animation is specialized in the creation of cinematic works, focusing on developing empathetic characters in fictional stories. Our vision is to create unique, empathetic characters within emotionally engaging stories.

History and Heritage

Daring is known as a bold and unconventional company, with a games-focused sub-brand, Daring Games. Its identity often features animal figures representing its daring nature, echoed in the slogan 'Are you bold enough?'. Daring Animation carries this audacity into its storytelling and brand.

Our Flagship Project: JESSE

After the unexpected death of his grandfather, Jesse loses his way and takes refuge in alcohol and drugs, leaving him with only the guitar given by his grandfather 14 years ago. While playing in a bar to survive, he is approached by a stranger who shows interest in one of the stickers on his instrument.

Project Mentorship: This short film was meticulously guided by professors Ana Isabel Veloso and Tânia Ribeiro.

Academic Context

The Daring Animation brand was created within the scope of the Marca e Portefólio (Brand and Portfolio) curricular unit, during the 3rd year of the Multimédia e Tecnologias da Comunicação degree at Universidade de Aveiro, under the supervision of professor Margarida Mouta.
